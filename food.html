🍴 FOOD HUNTING JOURNEY 🍜

Come and explore some of my favorite food spots!

Baeshake Cafe

Best place for korean food lovers because they have so many delicious korean foods. Most important, the price are affordable and have a big portions.

Delorong & Kopi Pak Ali

Perfect cafe for lunch dan dinner. They also have some desserts and they are open for any reservation occasions. .

Kopitan Classic White Coffee

Cozy vibes and authentic Malay food. These cafe located in the Amanjaya Mall at the ground floor.

Kedai Kopi Santapan

Best spot for breakfast because they served a traditional Malaysian breakfast and sometimes have pop up store sells kuih muih.

Makan Pagi by Fizzy Cafe

Sweet cozy place for breakfast also but in Penang. The foods is good and delicious and their services is tiptop. Even though you need to line up if it full house.

Ngopi@Kangar

Simple, aesthetic and vintage vibes. They have many kinds of sandwichs, pastries, and coffees.

Gelora Cafe

Sweet cozy place for healing. They have western and also Malay foods.

Oven Rimba Cafe

Sweet cozy place for cake & coffee at Perlis. You must go to this cafe if you are in Perlis.
