During my internship as an Information Management student, I had the opportunity to experience how record management is applied in a real workplace. I was assigned to several tasks such as organizing and updating staff files, preparing disposal forms, testing IT connection ports, and assisting in daily administrative work.

Throughout the internship, I learned how important proper documentation and record-keeping are in ensuring smooth operations. I also gained valuable communication and teamwork skills through working closely with staff and supervisors. This experience not only strengthened my understanding of Information Management concepts but also helped me grow more confident, responsible, and ready for future challenges in the professional world.
